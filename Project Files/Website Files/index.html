Abstract


The goal of the project " Airplane crash analysis using Tableau " is to thoroughly examine aviation accidents in order to determine what went wrong and develop better safety protocols. The project makes use of techniques including data cleansing, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, statistical analysis, and Tableau data visualization to use data from a variety of sources, including Kaggle datasets. The project's thorough crash study and practical suggestions are intended to promote cooperation within the aviation sector and promote ongoing advancements in safety standards. Stakeholders may benefit from a safer and more dependable aviation industry by putting these ideas into practice.
